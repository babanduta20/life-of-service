Project Highlights

Livelihoods & Food Security:

Trained 240 farmers and 80 women in climate-smart agriculture and support women-led enterprises in Kakuma Refugee Camp to drive inclusive economic growth.

Health & Emergency Response:

Delivered life-saving healthcare during the 2019 floods through mobile clinics, addressing malaria, waterborne diseases, and health education.

Gender Equality & Child Protection :

Trained over 700 women, 400 traditional leaders, and 240 child protection actors in GBV prevention and legal literacy. Reached 300 children with child rights education and contributed to reducing harmful practices like child marriage.

Education & Child Welfare:

Enrolled 116 refugee children, supported 205 children to complete school, and provided protection and psychosocial services to 400+ vulnerable children and widows.

WASH :

Reduced cholera and diarrheal diseases through improved water infrastructure, hygiene promotion, and community-led sanitation.

Peacebuilding & Social Cohesion:

Reduced intercommunal violence through integrated peace and livelihoods efforts. The Football4Peace program engaged 32 children in Jonglei State to promote unity, leadership, and healing through sport.

Climate Action:

Planted over 1,000 trees, trained 100+ youth climate ambassadors, and supported 25 local climate adaptation groups.

Volunteer Engagement:

Mobilized 420+ volunteers to serve 2,000+ youth and children, strengthening 30+ institutions through technical and capacity-building support.

Creative Healing & Trauma Recovery:

Promoted emotional healing in post-conflict communities through arts-based activities like poetry, drama, and roleplay.
